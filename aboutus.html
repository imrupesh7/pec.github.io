About Us

WELCOME TO PARGATI EYE CENTER

Pragati Eye Centre is the most trustable eye care and one of the best eye hospitals in Delhi. It is the result of our clear vision of providing high-quality services and a hassle-free environment to every patient that walks through our doors. With our dedicated team and cutting-edge technology provided at a very affordable rate, Pragati Eye Centre is the first choice for our patients when it comes to eye care.

Pragati Eye Centre is well-equipped with a qualified and experienced medical team led by Dr. Anil Biltoria, a renowned Ophthalmic Surgeon from the premier institute of India i.e. AIIMS. He has treated and operated on thousands of patients successfully in the last 17 years.

Since its establishment in 2009, Pragati Eye Centre has been at the forefront of technological breakthroughs in the field of ophthalmology. Our goal is to establish centres of excellence providing consistent high-level eye care using the best available surgical skills and technology.

TREATMENTS OFFERED AT PRAGATI EYE CENTRE

We are among the first few eye care centres to perform Lasik Surgery and Cataract Surgery in India by Topical Phacoemulsification (injection less / drop anesthesia) and now the Phaconit or Microincison Cataract Surgery in the country also called S-MICS.

In addition to providing laser treatment for eyes & cataract surgery, the centre provides comprehensive eye care diagnosing and treating medical and surgical diseases of the eye including Oculoplastic Surgery, Vitroretina, Glaucoma, Squint, etc. Pragati Eye Centre has complete vitreoretinal clinics equipped with both medical & surgical retina treatment facilities.
